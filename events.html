All You Need to Know About the Amara Hall of Fame Awards Night

Join us for an unforgettable celebration of brilliance, elegance, and inspiration as we honor outstanding achievements and visionary talents from across the globe. Here’s everything you need to make the most of this glamorous evening!

Date & Time

Date: Tuesday, August 12th, 2025

Time: Red Carpet opens at 6:00 PM

Mark your calendar for a night filled with star-studded moments, captivating performances, and inspirational stories.

Venue

Radio City Music Hall, New York

Address: 1260 Avenue of the Americas, New York, NY 10020

Iconic and grand, Radio City Music Hall is a symbol of elegance and entertainment excellence. Located in the heart of Manhattan, this historic venue is the perfect backdrop for a night dedicated to celebrating legends.

Historic Prestige

Known as the “Showplace of the Nation,” Radio City Music Hall brings a legacy of iconic events, adding distinction and exclusivity to any occasion.

Prime Location

Located in the heart of Midtown Manhattan, Radio City Music Hall offers easy access to nearby hotels, restaurants, and transportation for a seamless guest experience.

Unmatched Elegance

With its famous Art Deco design and rich history, the venue provides a stunning, sophisticated setting that elevates every event.

Dress Code

Dress to Impress – With a Touch of Glam!

Embrace sophistication and style as you step onto the red carpet in your finest evening attire. This is a night to be remembered, so dress with elegance and flair.
