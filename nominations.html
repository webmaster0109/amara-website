Categories

At the Amara Hall of Fame Awards, we honor those who dare to dream, redefine success, and inspire change. This is more than an awards ceremonyit is a tribute to excellence, vision, and artistry across borders. Each category pays homage to individuals of Indian origin and their global allies who have left an indelible mark on the world. Step into an atmosphere of elegance, mystery, and boundless achievement, where every accolade celebrates the spirit of greatness.
