About AHOFA

The Amara Hall of Fame Awards is a prestigious celebration dedicated to honoring the achievements of global Indian diaspora as well as international figures with strong ties with the Indian community.

AMARA

What Awaits You

  • Star-Studded Red Carpet:

    Be dazzled by the biggest names in entertainment, business, fashion, sports, technology, and more as they grace the red carpet.

  • Breathtaking Performances:

    Enjoy show-stopping performances from world-renowned artists that will make this night to remember.

  • Prestigious Awards:

    Witness your favorite Luminaries take center stage and receive accolades for their contributions to their industries.

  • Inspiration and Connection:

    Hear extraordinary stories of triumph and legacy while networking with global leaders and visionaries, fostering meaningful connections and opportunities for collaboration.

Why Attend?

This is more than just an event. Its a celebration of global overseas Indian talent, where legacies are honored, and futures are shaped. Whether youre looking for a glamorous night out or to witness the worlds biggest stars receive their accolades, this is the event of the year.
